## Context
A new school-year schedule for birthday celebrations needs to account for the Early May Bank Holiday (05/05/2025), which falls during term 5. Any birthdays landing on that Monday should instead be celebrated on Friday 2nd May so the cakes are ready before the long weekend.

## Requirements
- Input the data
- Update the input to use the 2024 Week 34 input
- Ensure that any birthdays on 5th May are rolled back to Friday 2nd May
- Watch out for duplicate rows!
- Output the data

## Output

- output_01.csv
  - 3 fields:
    - Cake Day
    - Cake Weekday
    - Count of Cakes
