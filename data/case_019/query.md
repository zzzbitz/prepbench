## Requirements

- Input the data
- Use the 2018 Tour de France results as your input
- Make all time fields seconds before doing any of the calculations
- Have seven or more riders complete the tour
- Must average 100 minutes or less behind the leader as a team
- Output the data

- output_01.csv
  - 3 fields:
    - Team Avg Gap in Mins
