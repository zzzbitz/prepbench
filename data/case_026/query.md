## Context
This week's challenge is to NOT USE TYPED CALCULATIONS in Prep to solve the challenge. No cheating with copying and pasting formulas either - just use Prep Builder's functionality.

## Requirements

- Input the data
- Name the columns 'Cocktails', 'Ingredient' and 'Price respectively
- Seperate the ingredients and create a single column of them
- Work out the position of the ingredients within the list of ingredients in each cocktail
- Work out the 'average price of the cocktails that ingredient is used in'
- Add the original cocktail price back in
- Output the data

## Output

- output_01.csv
  - 5 fields:
    - Ingredient Position
    - Ingredients Split
    - Cocktail Price
    - Cocktails
    - Avg Ingredient Price
