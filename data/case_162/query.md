## Context

Dealing with gaps in data sets is something any data prepper will have to deal with frequently. At the Prep School, some of our attendance recording isn't filled in well. Each lesson is taught at the same time, on the same day.

## Requirements

- Input the data
- Fill in the blanks
- Find the average Attendance per Lesson and Subject per weekday
- Output the data

## Output

- output_01.csv
  - 8 fields:
    - Weekday
    - Time
    - Week
    - Teacher
    - Subject
    - Lesson Name
    - Attendance
    - Avg. Attendance per Subject & Lesson
