## Context
We are looking at the worst selling products within 5 categories: home, groceries, beauty, electronics and clothes. We need to look at the total sales at a category level and compare the products within each category.

## Requirements
- Input the data
- Calculate the Total Sales Value for each Product
- Calculate the Total Sales at a Category level
- Work out the Percentage of Total for each Product within a Category
- Identify the products which make up the bottom 15% of each category's sales and filter the data to just these products
- Remove unnecessary fields
- Output the data

## Output

- output_01.csv
  - 6 fields:
    - Category
    - Product
    - Product ID
    - Price
    - Sales
    - % of Total
